Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

MatchOracle respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the MatchOracle mobile application and related website handle information.

1. Overview

MatchOracle is a football information app that provides fixtures, live match context, statistics, team form, and prediction-style insights.

MatchOracle is provided for informational and entertainment purposes only.

MatchOracle does not provide betting services, does not allow users to place wagers, does not operate as a bookmaker, sportsbook, casino, gambling platform, or real-money gaming service, and does not handle deposits, withdrawals, or payouts.

The current version of MatchOracle does not require users to create an account and does not process payments.

2. Information We Do Not Require

In the current version, MatchOracle does not require you to provide:

  • your name;
  • postal address;
  • phone number;
  • email address for account registration;
  • account login credentials;
  • payment details;
  • profile information.

MatchOracle does not currently provide user accounts, user profiles, chat, comments, or user-generated content features.

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store limited app-related preferences and app state locally on your device, such as:

  • selected language;
  • selected timezone;
  • favorite matches or teams;
  • notification preferences;
  • viewed or opened prediction cards;
  • cached football content used to improve app performance.

This information is used to make the app work correctly, remember your preferences, and improve the in-app experience.

Local app data may remain on your device until you delete the app, clear the relevant app settings, or your device removes the data according to its operating system behavior.

4. Information Processed by Our Services

To provide football fixtures, live match context, match details, statistics, team form, prediction-style insights, and performance summaries, the app may communicate with MatchOracle backend services.

These requests may include football-related parameters such as match ID, date, league or competition filters, and similar technical request details.

Like most internet services, our backend and hosting providers may process technical information such as:

  • IP address;
  • request time;
  • endpoint path;
  • request headers;
  • device or browser metadata;
  • app version and build metadata;
  • error logs and server logs.

This information may be used for app operation, security, debugging, abuse prevention, rate limiting, service reliability, and performance monitoring.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, MatchOracle may use notifications to provide app-related football updates and reminders.

Depending on your settings, notification-related information may include:

  • Apple Push Notification service device token;
  • notification preferences;
  • selected notification event types;
  • timezone identifier;
  • favorite match IDs used to provide relevant notifications.

You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in your device settings.

The current version does not use notifications to request payments or purchases.

6. Third-Party Services

MatchOracle may use third-party services for football data, hosting, backend infrastructure, caching, rate limiting, and related technical functionality.

These services may include:

  • football data providers;
  • hosting providers;
  • backend infrastructure providers;
  • cache and rate-limiting providers.

These services may process technical request data as part of normal internet operation and service delivery.

MatchOracle does not sell personal information.

MatchOracle does not use personal information for advertising tracking.

MatchOracle does not include advertising, attribution, or tracking SDKs in the current version based on the current release audit.

7. Payments and Purchases

The current version of MatchOracle does not process payments, does not sell digital units, and does not provide active in-app purchases.

Prediction reveal is free in the current version.

If payments, digital units, subscriptions, or in-app purchases are introduced in a future version, this Privacy Policy may be updated as needed.

8. Children’s Privacy

MatchOracle is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will review the request.

9. Data Retention

Local app preferences may remain on your device until you delete the app or reset the relevant settings.

Technical server logs may be retained for a limited period for security, debugging, reliability, abuse prevention, and operational purposes.

Cached football data and prediction-related technical records may be retained as needed to operate, improve, debug, and maintain the service.

Where possible, MatchOracle limits stored information to what is reasonably necessary for app functionality, security, and reliability.

10. Your Choices

You can:

  • disable notifications in the app or device settings;
  • remove local app data by deleting the app;
  • avoid using optional favorites or notification features;
  • contact us with privacy-related questions.

11. International Processing

MatchOracle services and third-party providers may process technical information in different countries or regions depending on hosting, infrastructure, and service-provider operations.

By using MatchOracle, you understand that technical information may be processed where our service providers operate.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If we make changes, we will update the effective date on this page.

Your continued use of MatchOracle after changes means that you acknowledge the updated Privacy Policy.
